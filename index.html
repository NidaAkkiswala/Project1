A Short Memorable Travel Story

I love to travel around and exploring new activities in differnet places.

I've only been to two places outside of the United States which is Saudia Arabia, India, and Canada.

My family and a few family friends recently traveled to Gatlinburg, Tennessee by car.

The most enjoyable part of the trip was hiking up the mountains with my family zero gear and discovering small streams of water.

On our last day there we decided to have a barbecue by a small stream with a moderate current. It was all going well till it started raining, but was that going to stop my desi family from barbecueing, no it was not. It didn't rain long but it was enough to wet some of the food we couldn't cover.

It is said that you shouldn't swim in the stream as the current could be fast but again was that going to stop a whole bunch of kids from wading to the middle of the stream where a big huge rock was, again no it wasn't. The only casualty we had was my one of my cousin's slides sliding off his foot and into the river.

Overall it was the best trip I've had so far.
